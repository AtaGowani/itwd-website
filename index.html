HTML, CSS, and JS

No previous knowledge is required! The course will start off with the basics and build up. You will get to practice your skills with projects throughout the course. Alongside, you will also get exposure to Git and Github for version control and will get familiarity with Bootstrap 4, world's most popular framework for building websites.

Get hands-on experience!

It's not just a course. You will get the chance to make an impact with the skills you acquire. You, as a student, will be working in a team to help a non-profit organization with their web-related technical needs and see your creation come to life.

Forty hours of class time

Students will be meeting twice per week for eight weeks over the summer at The University of Texas at Dallas. The duration of the course will cover the material to be taught, in-class projects, and the hands-on project for the non-profit organization.

FAQ

I don't have any web development experience, is that okay?

Yes, that is totally fine! The course starts off at the very basics and builds on from there.

Where will the course take place?

Classes will be held at The University of Texas at Dallas.

How much does it cost?

$150

Will there be food?

Yes! Breakfast, lunch, and snacks will be provided each day.

Do I need to bring in anything?

You will need to bring in your laptop every day. It is also recommended that you bring in a notebook with you to take notes on.

Who can apply for this course?

This course is aimed at high school students, but college students will also be considered.

Why do I need to apply?

There's only a limited amount of spots and we want to give everyone a fair chance to be considered.

How many spots are there?

At the end of the selection process ten students will be accepted.

What is the selection process based off of?

The goal of this course is to give young minds a chance to experience web development and learn it's basics. We are looking for people who are passionate about learning web development and will be team players for our final project.

I don't see my quesiton here. What do I do?

Email us at humans@itwd.us and we will get back to you with an answer!
