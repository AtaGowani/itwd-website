Your Project + Our Students

When you submit a project, we will evaluate your needs and see if they are well-suited for our students to work on. If your project is selected, our students will work with you to meet your needs and make your project come to life. We've developed this system to gives our students the chance to work on a real-world project and also help non-profits further their cause through effective use of technology.

Summer 2018 Projects

Due to the specific content being covered in the course there are specific types of project that we prefer over others. The types of project that we are looking for this summer are projects that incorperate the use of HTML, CSS, and JS. This could be anywhere from building a website from scratch to implementing a new front-end feature on the website. If you're not sure whether your project would meet our criteria just submit your project and mention that when describing your project. We'll be sure to reach out and clear up any confusion.
